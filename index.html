AVODA (AVD)

Myanmar’s Digital Currency for Financial Freedom

Introduction

AVODA is a fast, borderless, and transparent digital currency built to empower Myanmar's economy and people. It offers a solution to inflation, slow payments, and lack of financial inclusion.
